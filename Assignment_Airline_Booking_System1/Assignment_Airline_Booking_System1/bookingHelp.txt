Steps:

1. If you wan one way flight click on "One destination" button,
and if you want to book a second leg, onward from the first destination 
or back to the original departure airport click on "Two destinations" button.

2. You can choose desired flight and date. 

3. You can choose desired type of seat.

4. Write your name and click on "OK" button to book flight.
This screen will lead you to confirmation screen where you can check 
your details and confirm if you want to proceed or exit if you changed 
you mind.

5. Click on "Exit" button to return to main menu.

Caution:

Chosen date needs to be from tomorrow and other dates can be the same
or they need to be in future respectively.
